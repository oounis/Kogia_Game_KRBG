## In-game terminology (English/Roman - what players actually see)
- **The Citadel** - the empty centre square (left empty during placement; the first move enters it).
- **Placement** - the opening phase where both sides set out their soldiers, two at a time.
- **The Exchange** - the 2-for-1 trade of an unreachable soldier.
- **Soldiers** - the pieces (traditionally nicknamed "the dogs" locally; we brand them as Soldiers, per the Roman root).
- **Centurion** - a Soldier promoted after 7 captures (Origins Mode - see 12-Innovation-Lab).

How to play

Tap a soldier, then tap a highlighted square. Trap an enemy between two of yours on a line to capture it.

How to play

Move

Slide a soldier one square — up, down, left or right. Never diagonally.

Surround

Trap an enemy between two of yours on a line, and it's captured.

Multi-capture

A single move can capture several enemies at once.

Win

Capture the enemy down to one piece — or block them entirely.

The rules, in full

  • The board. 7×7. Two armies of soldiers; the centre square (the Citadel) starts empty.
  • Moving. One soldier per turn moves a single square orthogonally into an empty cell.
  • Capturing. Move so an enemy is sandwiched between two of your soldiers on a row or column — it's removed. A move may trap several at once.
  • Safe. A soldier that moves itself between two enemies is not captured.
  • Winning. Reduce the enemy to one soldier, or leave them with no legal move.
  • Pure skill. No dice, no cards, no luck — descended from the Roman Ludus Latrunculorum.

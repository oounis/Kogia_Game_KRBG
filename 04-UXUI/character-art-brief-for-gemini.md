## 1) The project
I'm building a mobile game called **"Kharbga: Origins"** by my studio **Kogia Games**.
Kharbga is a 2,500-year-old traditional strategy board game from southern Tunisia — a descendant of the Roman
game *Ludus Latrunculorum* ("the game of soldiers"). It's a pure-skill, no-luck game (like chess/checkers):
two players move pieces on a 7×7 board and capture an enemy by surrounding it between two of their own.

I'm turning it into a fun, colourful mobile game. The pieces are **"dog-soldiers"** — cute little dogs dressed
as ancient soldiers (a playful nod to the fact the traditional pieces were nicknamed "the dogs"). There are
**two armies**. When a piece captures 7 enemies it is promoted to a crowned **"Centurion."**
I need character + asset art for the game's website and app store.

## 2) The art style — KEEP THIS IDENTICAL FOR EVERY IMAGE
- Cute, friendly, premium **3D-rendered character** look — **Pixar / modern-Smurfs-movie quality**.
- Smooth rounded shapes, soft studio lighting, big expressive friendly eyes, warm and family-friendly.
- Each **character on a transparent background (PNG)**, **full body**, facing slightly 3/4 to camera, heroic pose.
- **Same proportions, same style, same lighting** across all characters (they must look like one collection).
- High resolution (1024×1024 or larger).

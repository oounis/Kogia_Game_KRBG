## Part 1 — The project
I'm building a mobile game, **"Kharbga: Origins"** by **Kogia Games**. Kharbga is a 2,500-year-old strategy
board game from North Africa, descended from the **Roman game *Ludus Latrunculorum*** ("the game of
soldiers"). It is a vintage, historical, pure-skill game (like chess) played on a **7×7 board**: two armies
move and capture by surrounding the enemy. Traditionally it was played with **two colours of stones/pebbles**.
When a piece captures **7 enemies**, it is promoted to a powerful crowned **"Centurion."**

I want an **attractive, epic-but-friendly Roman/ancient-world look — inspired by Age of Empires** (warm,
historical, painterly, grand) but **cute and game-friendly**, not grim or too serious.

## Part 2 — THE AVATAR CONCEPT (very important — read carefully)
**The pieces are NOT dogs, NOT humans, NOT normal animals.** They are a **unique creature invented for this
game: living "Stone Warriors"** — adorable little **sentient pebble/stone soldiers**, as if the ancient game
stones came to life. This fits the real history (the game was played with stones) and is 100% unique.

Design of a Stone Warrior:
- A **cute, rounded pebble/boulder body** (chibi proportions, like a friendly mobile-game mascot), carved
  from stone, with **two big sparkly friendly eyes** and a small confident smile.
- **Tiny stubby stone arms and legs.**
- Wears a small **Roman soldier helmet (galea) with a crest**, and carries a **tiny round shield**.
- Two armies, distinguished by **stone type**:
  - **Team SAND** = warm pale **sandstone** body, **bronze/gold** helmet with a **red** crest.
  - **Team STONE** = cool dark **basalt/granite** body, **steel** helmet with a **teal/blue** crest.
- Charming, premium **3D-rendered look (Pixar / Clash-Royale-mascot quality)**, soft studio lighting, smooth.

## Part 3 — RULES for every image (STYLE LOCK)
- **Premium 3D render**, cute and friendly, warm Roman/desert colours, soft studio lighting, smooth shading.
- **Each asset is its OWN separate image** with a **transparent background** (or flat solid colour I can cut out).
- **Full body**, slight **3/4 angle**, centered, high resolution (≥1024px), consistent scale & proportions.

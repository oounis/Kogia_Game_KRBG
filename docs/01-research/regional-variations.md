# Kharbga — Regional Variations & Field-Research Plan

> The game has many local forms. Cataloguing them is part of the cultural-preservation mission and gives
> us authentic content (and future game modes). This file tracks what to collect and verify.

## Piece materials (heritage authenticity for art & store themes)
- Two colours of **stones / pebbles**
- **Date seeds** (نواة التمر)
- **White shells** (البابوش الأبيض)
- **Bottle caps** (modern improvisation)

→ These become **unlockable board/piece themes** in the game store (heritage = monetisation that fits the brand).

- [ ] Note local terminology (Malha, التعمير, الحاف, الكلاب) and pronunciation.

- [ ] Cross-check the 5×5 "kids" variant against Seega.

## Terminology (keep authentic in-game)
- **المالحة (Malha)** — the empty centre square.
- **التعمير (Ta'mir)** — the placement phase.
- **الحاف (Al-Haf)** — the 2-for-1 exchange.
- **الكلاب (al-kilab, "the dogs")** — the traditional name for the pieces. *(Brand reframes them as
  "soldiers" — acknowledge this as a deliberate choice, see game-design.)*

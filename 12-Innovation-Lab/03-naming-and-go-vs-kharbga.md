# Naming & "Is Kharbga like Go?"

## Part 1 — Is Kharbga really similar to Go?
**Partly — but it's actually closer to chess/checkers than to Go.** Here's the honest comparison:

| | **Go** | **Kharbga** | **Chess** |
|---|--------|-------------|-----------|
| Pieces move after placing? | ❌ (mostly static) | ✅ **yes, you move** | ✅ yes |
| Capture by… | surrounding (removing all liberties) | **sandwiching** between 2 (custodial) | attacking the square |
| Goal | control **territory** | **eliminate / block** the enemy | checkmate the king |
| Piece types | 1 (stones) | 1 (soldiers) | 6 |
| Luck | none | none | none |

**Verdict:** Kharbga *looks* Go-ish (grid + surround + no luck), but mechanically it's a **move-and-capture**
game — the Latrunculorum/checkers family. The Go feeling you sense is the **"surround to win" DNA**. 
👉 Good news for your plan: borrowing **one** thing from each is the sweet spot —
**Go's territory idea** (the Malha-as-prize, experiment D) + **chess's royal target & piece variety** (A, B).
That blend is exactly what makes "Origins Mode" feel fresh yet familiar.

## Part 2 — A short, memorable, global name
You want something easy like "Chess" or "Go." Reality check first: **don't over-optimize the name now** — a
great game with an "ok" name beats the reverse, and you can decide closer to launch. But here are strong options:

### Direction 1 — keep Kharbga + a global subtitle (recommended for heritage)
- **Kharbga: Origins** ⭐ (your pick — keeps authenticity + SEO + "preservation" story)
- Kharbga: Legends · Kharbga: Sands
- *Pro:* protects the cultural mission. *Con:* "Kharbga" is hard for non-Arabic speakers to say/spell.

### Direction 2 — a short coined brand (easy like "Go")
Pulled from the game's own world, so it stays authentic:
- **MALHA** ⭐ — the golden center square; 2 syllables, easy, evocative, *ownable* (no one uses it), and it
  literally names the heart of the board. Strong candidate.
- **SIGA** — the ancient name of the Seega family; ultra-short, but more generic.
- **NUMIDA / NUMID** — from the Numidian desert warriors; sounds epic, global.
- **KSAR** — the fortified granaries of Tataouine; strong, short, visual.

### Direction 3 — descriptive global (easiest discovery, least authentic)
- Desert Strategy · Sahara Tactics · Nomad Kings · Trap (the core verb)

### My recommendation
Use a **two-layer brand**: a short hero name + heritage anchor.
> **MALHA** — *the ancient game of Kharbga* (logo: مالحة / خربقة)
or simply commit to **Kharbga: Origins** if you want the heritage name to lead.
Decide at launch; for now keep the repo name and revisit with real audience feedback (A/B test on the website).
